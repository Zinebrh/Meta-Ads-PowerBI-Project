# 📊 Meta Ads Performance Analysis  
### 🚀 Power BI Dashboard Project  

---

## 🌍 Overview  

This project presents a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze advertising performance across **Facebook** and **Instagram**.  

🎯 The objective is to transform campaign data into **clear, actionable insights** to improve marketing performance and maximize ROI.  

---

## 💼 Business Context  

Companies invest heavily in digital advertising but often lack visibility on what truly drives performance.  

This dashboard helps answer key questions:  

- 📌 Which platform performs better: Facebook or Instagram?  
- 👥 Which audience segments are the most engaged?  
- 🎯 Which ad formats generate the best results?  
- ⏰ When are users most active?  
- 💰 How efficient is the conversion funnel?  

---

## ⚙️ Dashboard Capabilities  

The dashboard provides an **interactive experience** that allows users to:  

- 🔄 Switch between Facebook and Instagram views  
- 👩‍👩‍👧 Analyze audience by gender and age  
- 🌎 Explore performance by country  
- 📈 Identify peak engagement periods  
- 🎥 Compare ad formats and campaign effectiveness  

---

## 📊 Key Insights  

- ✅ Strong engagement and visibility (high CTR & Engagement Rate)  
- ⚠️ Low conversion performance (funnel inefficiency)  
- 👩 Females aged **18–30** are the most engaged  
- ⏰ Peak activity in **afternoon & evening**  
- 🎥 Video & Story ads outperform other formats  
- 🌍 Top markets: India, US, Brazil  

---

## 💡 Recommendations  

- 🎯 Focus on high-performing audience segments  
- 🎥 Invest more in Video and Story ads  
- ⏰ Optimize ad scheduling  
- 🔧 Improve landing pages & conversion strategy  
- 🌎 Allocate budget based on geographic performance
